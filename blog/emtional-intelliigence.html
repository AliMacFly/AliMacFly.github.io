The differnce between IQ and EQ

IQ is academic intelligence skills that can be taught and scored to measure your mental abilities to complete practical tasks. EQ is emotional intelligence also known as emotional quotient, it is a skill of understanding and managing your emotions.

Describe why EQ is important and how it is relevant to you

EQ is important because knowing how to control one’s emotions under stress or discomfort helps deter conflicts, builds relationships, and creates a comfortable working environment for others and yourself. Engage and practice mindfulness

Practicing mindfulness is being aware of one’s feelings in the moment. Breathing techniques like meditation is one way to practice mindfulness, focusing on breathing helps reduce stress or frustration which relaxes the mind and body.
